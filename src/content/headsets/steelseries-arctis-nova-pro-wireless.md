## Overview

The SteelSeries Arctis Nova Pro Wireless stands as a flagship headset that attempts to do it all. It combines high-fidelity audio with active noise cancellation and a unique hot-swappable battery system that ensures you never have to stop gaming to charge.

## Key Features

The Infinity Power System is the standout feature here. The headset comes with two batteries and a charging station in the wireless base station. While using one battery, the other charges in the base, allowing for near-infinite wireless gameplay.

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) uses a four-mic hybrid system to tune out background noise, while Transparency Mode lets you hear your surroundings when needed.

Audio quality is top-tier with high-fidelity drivers capable of High-Res Audio on PC/PS5. The connection is handled by the Multi-System Connect base station, which lets you switch between two platforms (like PC and PlayStation) with the press of a button.

## Comfort

The ComfortMAX System includes height-adjustable rotating earcups, a flexible suspension band, and glasses-friendly design. It's built with premium PVD-coated steel improved over previous generations.

## Verdict

If budget is no object, the Arctis Nova Pro Wireless is one of the most complete packages on the market.
