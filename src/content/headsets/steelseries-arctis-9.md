## Overview
The SteelSeries Arctis 9 offers the ultimate wireless flexibility with simultaneous 2.4GHz and Bluetooth connectivity. This premium headset allows you to connect to your gaming PC while staying connected to your phone, ensuring you never miss important calls during gaming sessions.

## Key Features
- Dual wireless: 2.4GHz for gaming + Bluetooth for mobile
- Simultaneous audio mixing from both connections
- Retractable ClearCast microphone with studio clarity
- 20-hour battery life with quick charge capability
- ChatMix dial for balancing game and chat audio

## Specifications
The Arctis 9 features 40mm drivers with SteelSeries' signature audio clarity, delivering 7.1 surround sound with 20Hz-20kHz frequency response and 32-ohm impedance. Weighing 358 grams, it provides all-day comfort with AirWeave ear cushions. The dual wireless system allows simultaneous connection to gaming devices via 2.4GHz and mobile devices via Bluetooth.
