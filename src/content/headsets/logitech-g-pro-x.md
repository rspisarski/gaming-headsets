## Overview
The Logitech G PRO X is designed in collaboration with professional esports players, delivering exceptional audio performance and voice clarity. With Blue VO!CE microphone technology and DTS Headphone:X 2.0 surround sound, this headset provides the competitive edge needed for professional gaming.

## Key Features
- Blue VO!CE microphone technology with real-time voice filters
- DTS Headphone:X 2.0 surround sound for precise positional audio
- PRO-G 50mm drivers for clear and accurate sound reproduction
- Detachable professional gaming microphone with noise reduction
- Steel and aluminum construction for durability

## Specifications
The G PRO X features Logitech's PRO-G 50mm drivers with a frequency response of 20Hz-20kHz and 39-ohm impedance. The headset weighs 320 grams and includes both leatherette and velour ear cushions for comfort. The Blue VO!CE technology offers real-time voice filters including noise reduction, compressor, and de-esser for professional broadcast quality audio.
