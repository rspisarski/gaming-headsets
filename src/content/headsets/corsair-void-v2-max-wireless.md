## Overview

The Corsair Void v2 MAX Wireless builds upon the foundation of the popular Void Wireless V2, adding premium features that justify its $149.99 price point. Released in late 2025, this enhanced model introduces simultaneous dual wireless connectivity, Sonarworks SoundID audio personalization, and improved acoustic tuning while maintaining the lightweight comfort and exceptional battery life that made its predecessor a hit.

At its core, the Void v2 MAX Wireless addresses key requests from the gaming community by allowing simultaneous connections to multiple devices. Gamers can now stay connected to their console or PC via 2.4GHz wireless while also maintaining a Bluetooth connection to their smartphone for calls, music, or voice chat appsâ€”all without switching between sources manually.

## Key Features

**Simultaneous Dual Wireless**: Unlike the standard Void V2, the MAX version supports simultaneous 2.4GHz and Bluetooth connections, allowing you to hear audio from both your gaming device and smartphone at the same time. Take calls or listen to music while gaming without missing in-game audio.

**Sonarworks SoundID Integration**: The MAX version includes access to Sonarworks SoundID technology through the iCUE software, creating personalized audio profiles based on your unique hearing characteristics and preferences. This professional audio calibration tool helps tailor the headset's output to your exact needs.

**Enhanced Dolby Atmos Tuning**: While both models support Dolby Atmos, the MAX receives additional acoustic tuning specifically optimized for spatial audio performance. The custom-tuned 50mm drivers benefit from improved passive isolation with denser foam padding around the drivers.

**Industry-Leading Battery Life**: Maintains the impressive battery performance of the standard V2 with up to 70 hours via 2.4GHz wireless or 130 hours via Bluetooth. Fast charging delivers 6 hours of use from a quick 15-minute charge, minimizing downtime.

**Premium Audio Features**: Access advanced audio customization through Corsair iCUE software with multiple EQ presets, custom equalizer settings, and the exclusive Sonarworks SoundID personalization that sets this model apart from its more affordable sibling.

## Audio Quality

The Void v2 MAX Wireless features the same 50mm neodymium drivers as the standard V2 but benefits from refined acoustic tuning and improved passive noise isolation. The enhanced padding around the drivers creates a better seal, reducing ambient noise bleed and improving bass response slightly over the standard model.

The sound signature remains V-shaped with elevated bass and treble, but the MAX's improved tuning delivers cleaner separation between frequency ranges. Explosions hit with satisfying impact while footsteps and environmental cues remain distinct and easy to locate. The Dolby Atmos implementation creates convincing spatial audio that enhances both competitive gaming and cinematic single-player experiences.

The inclusion of Sonarworks SoundID is a standout feature, allowing users to create personalized audio profiles that compensate for individual hearing differences and preferences. This professional-grade calibration tool typically found in studio equipment adds genuine value for audiophile gamers willing to invest time in proper setup.

## Comfort & Build

Physically identical to the standard Void Wireless V2, the MAX version weighs 303 grams and features the same lightweight plastic construction. The key difference lies in slightly denser memory foam cushions and additional padding around the drivers, which improves the seal and passive isolation without significantly affecting comfort.

The breathable microfiber fabric covering prevents heat buildup during extended sessions, while the flexible headband accommodates various head sizes comfortably. The 90-degree swiveling ear cups and adjustable design ensure a secure fit for most users, including glasses wearers. The mesh fabric promotes airflow, keeping your ears cool even during marathon gaming sessions.

## Verdict

The Corsair Void v2 MAX Wireless sits in the sweet spot between mid-range and premium wireless gaming headsets at $149.99. The $20 premium over the standard Void Wireless V2 buys you genuinely useful features: simultaneous dual wireless connectivity, Sonarworks SoundID personalization, and enhanced acoustic tuning that elevates the listening experience.

For gamers who frequently multitask between gaming and other devices, or those who appreciate professional audio calibration tools, the MAX version represents excellent value. The simultaneous connection feature alone changes how you interact with your devices, eliminating the frustration of switching between gaming and mobile audio sources. However, if you primarily game on a single platform and don't need advanced audio customization, the standard Void Wireless V2 offers 95% of the experience at a lower price point.

The Void v2 MAX Wireless excels as a versatile, feature-rich wireless headset that punches above its weight class. Its combination of comfort, battery life, multi-platform compatibility, and premium audio features makes it an outstanding choice for gamers seeking a single headset solution across their entire gaming ecosystem.
