## Overview
The SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 delivers premium wireless gaming audio with impressive battery life and versatile connectivity options. Featuring SteelSeries' Nova Acoustic Sound System with custom 40mm drivers, this headset provides clear, detailed audio across all gaming platforms.

## Key Features
- 38-hour battery life with rapid charge capability
- Simultaneous 2.4GHz wireless and Bluetooth connectivity
- Retractable broadcast-grade microphone with AI noise cancellation
- Comfortable AirWeave ear cushions with adjustable ski-goggle headband
- On-ear audio controls for easy adjustments

## Specifications
The Arctis Nova 7 uses 40mm neodymium drivers with a frequency response of 20Hz-20kHz and 32-ohm impedance. The headset weighs 315 grams and features a retractable microphone with sidetone control. Battery life reaches 38 hours with RGB lighting enabled, or up to 42 hours with lighting disabled.
