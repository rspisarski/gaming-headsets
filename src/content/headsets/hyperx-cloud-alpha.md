## Overview
The HyperX Cloud Alpha sets the standard for wired gaming headsets with its innovative dual chamber driver technology. This design separates bass frequencies from mids and highs, resulting in clearer audio and reduced distortion during intense gaming sessions.

## Key Features
- Dual chamber 50mm drivers for improved audio clarity
- Durable aluminum frame with signature HyperX comfort
- Detachable noise-cancelling microphone with pop filter
- Memory foam ear cushions with leatherette alternatives
- Multi-platform compatibility via 3.5mm connection

## Specifications
The Cloud Alpha features 50mm dual chamber drivers with a wide frequency response of 13Hz-27kHz and 65-ohm impedance. Weighing 322 grams, this headset offers all-day comfort with its padded headband and memory foam ear cushions. The detachable microphone provides clear voice communication with effective background noise reduction.
