## Overview
The ASTRO Gaming A50 represents premium wireless gaming audio with Dolby Audio processing and exceptional build quality. Designed for serious gamers who demand the best in wireless performance and comfort, the A50 delivers theater-quality surround sound in a stylish, comfortable package.

## Key Features
- Dolby Audio surround sound for immersive gaming experience
- 15-hour battery life with optional base station charging
- Mod kit ready with replaceable ear cushions and headband
- Flip-to-mute microphone with broadcast quality audio
- Comfortable fit for extended gaming sessions

## Specifications
The A50 features 40mm drivers with Dolby Audio processing, delivering 7.1 surround sound with a frequency response of 20Hz-20kHz and 48-ohm impedance. The headset weighs 370 grams and offers up to 15 hours of wireless battery life. The base station provides both charging and audio processing while keeping your headset ready for action.
