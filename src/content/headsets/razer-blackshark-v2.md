## Overview
The Razer BlackShark V2 delivers tournament-grade audio performance in a lightweight, comfortable design. Featuring THX Spatial Audio and advanced passive noise cancellation, this headset is engineered for competitive gaming where audio clarity and positional awareness are crucial.

## Key Features
- THX 7.1 surround sound for precise positional audio
- Advanced passive noise cancellation with earcup design
- Lightweight 240g construction for extended gaming sessions
- Detachable cardioid microphone with voice clarity optimization
- TriForce 50mm drivers for crisp highs and deep bass

## Specifications
The BlackShark V2 utilizes Razer's TriForce 50mm drivers with a frequency response of 12Hz-28kHz and 32-ohm impedance. Weighing only 240 grams, it features memory foam ear cushions with breathable fabric. The detachable cardioid microphone provides clear voice communication with effective background noise reduction through its acoustic chamber design.
