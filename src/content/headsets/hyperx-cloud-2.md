## Overview
The HyperX Cloud II builds upon the original Cloud's success with enhanced comfort, improved audio quality, and convenient features. This headset delivers premium sound quality through its 53mm drivers while maintaining the comfort that made the original a favorite among gamers.

## Key Features
- 53mm drivers with enhanced bass response
- 7.1 virtual surround sound via USB sound card
- Detachable noise-cancelling microphone
- Memory foam ear cushions with leatherette option
- Aluminum frame for durability and lightweight design

## Specifications
The Cloud II features 53mm drivers with a wide frequency response of 15Hz-25kHz and 60-ohm impedance. Weighing 350 grams, it includes both leatherette and velour ear cushions for different comfort preferences. The included USB sound card provides 7.1 virtual surround sound and independent audio and microphone volume controls.
